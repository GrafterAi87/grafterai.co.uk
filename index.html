How it works

Set up once.
Works forever.

01

Customer sends an enquiry

Grafter AI replies within minutes — day or night. It responds professionally, gives a rough guide price range so there are no nasty surprises, and asks for photos or details ready for your visit.

Never miss a lead again
02

You go and see the job

For jobs that need a site visit, Grafter books it straight into your Google Calendar. You go, assess the work, and decide your price. Your expertise stays yours — Grafter just handles everything around it.

Your skills. Your prices. Always.
03

Send a professional quote in seconds

Trade-specific templates pre-loaded for your job type. Tap the template, adjust the price if needed, and Grafter sends a polished formal quote. Customers see a proper business. You spent 30 seconds.

Templates built for your trade
04

Invoices, reminders and reviews — all handled

Job done? Grafter sends the invoice, requests the deposit, chases if unpaid after 7 days, and asks for a Checkatrade review automatically. You don't touch any of it.

Get paid faster
05

Daily summary — always in control

Every evening a plain-English summary of what Grafter did that day. New leads, quotes sent, money received, jobs booked. Everything in one place, nothing missed.
